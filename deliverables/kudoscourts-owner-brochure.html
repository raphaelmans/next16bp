Reservation System
Flexible, not rigid.

The system adapts to how you actually operate. Tap a scenario to see how it works.

Pending player requests → you accept → Confirmed

A player finds your court and requests a booking. You see it in your inbox and tap Accept — done. The booking is confirmed instantly. No payment step because the court is free.

You have a 45-minute window to accept or reject. If you don't respond, the request expires automatically and the slot opens back up.
Pending → Awaiting Payment → Payment Marked → Confirmed

A player requests a paid court. You accept, which starts a 45-minute payment window. The player pays you directly (GCash, Maya, bank transfer) and marks it in-app. You tap Confirm Payment to finalize.

Every step is timed. If the player doesn't pay in time, the reservation expires and the slot reopens. No manual cleanup needed.
Pending player already paid you directly → Confirm Paid Offline → Confirmed

A player requests a booking but they already paid you directly — cash at the counter, GCash, or bank transfer outside the app. Instead of going through the payment flow, you tap Confirm Paid Offline in one action. The booking jumps straight to confirmed.

This is for your suki, walk-ins who already paid, or anyone who handles payment through your traditional channels. The system records it for your audit trail without forcing them through the app.
You Create Booking → Confirmed

A regular customer calls, messages on Viber, or shows up in person. You open the Availability Studio, pick the time, select their guest profile — and the booking is immediately confirmed. No app needed on their end. No request, no payment flow.

Guest bookings keep your schedule accurate for all customers — not just those who use the app. Everything in one place.

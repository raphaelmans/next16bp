### Reservation System — Flexible, Not Rigid

An inbox-style dashboard where you manage all bookings. But the system isn't one-size-fits-all — it adapts to how you actually operate:

- **Free courts:** A player requests, you accept — confirmed instantly.
- **Paid courts (online):** A player requests → you accept → they pay via GCash/Maya/bank → you confirm payment. Clear steps, timed deadlines, no guesswork.
- **Paid courts (offline):** A player requests, but they already paid you directly (cash, GCash, bank transfer)? You can confirm paid offline in one action — skip the payment flow entirely.
- **Guest bookings:** A regular customer calls or messages you directly? Create a confirmed booking for them from the Availability Studio. No app needed on their end.

Every reservation has a clear status — Pending, Awaiting Payment, Payment Marked, Confirmed, Cancelled, or Expired — so you always know where things stand. Nothing gets lost.

### Availability Studio

A visual timeline where you see and control your entire schedule. Desktop shows a full week view; mobile shows one day at a time. You manage everything by placing blocks:

- **Available slots** (green, dashed) — Open time that players can book through the platform.
- **Maintenance blocks** (gray) — Block off time for repairs, cleaning, or private events. Players can't book these slots.
- **Walk-in blocks** (light orange, dashed) — Reserve time for walk-in customers. When someone shows up and pays, convert the block into a confirmed guest booking with one tap.
- **Guest bookings** (medium orange) — Create a confirmed reservation directly for a customer — no app involvement on their side. Perfect for regulars, phone bookings, or anyone who pays you through traditional channels.
- **Booked** (strong orange) — A player booked this slot through the app. The booking went through the full flow — request, acceptance, payment confirmation.

All blocks prevent double-booking automatically. The system knows what's taken and only shows players what's actually available. Each block type has a distinct color so you can read your schedule at a glance — green means open, gray means blocked, and orange shades show different reservation states.

### Booking Imports

Already have bookings in spreadsheets, calendars, or even screenshots? Import them directly. Our AI-powered normalizer maps your existing data so you don't start from zero.

Supported formats: CSV, Excel (XLSX), iCal (ICS), and image uploads.

### Guest Profiles — For Your Regulars and Walk-Ins

Not every customer uses the app — and that's fine. Create guest profiles for your suki (regulars), phone bookers, and walk-in customers. Save their name, phone, and email once, then reuse the profile every time they book.

How it works in practice:

- **Suki calls to reserve?** Create a guest booking with their profile — immediately confirmed, no app needed on their end. They pay you directly like they always have.
- **Walk-in arrives?** If you pre-set a walk-in block, convert it to a guest booking when they check in. The block becomes a confirmed reservation tied to their profile.
- **New walk-in?** Create a guest profile on the spot and book them in — all in one step.

This means your schedule stays accurate whether bookings come through the app, by phone, or at the front desk. Everything lives in one place.

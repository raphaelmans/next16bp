# KudosCourts

## The Problem
- Court discovery is fragmented across social pages, calls, and spreadsheets
- Availability is unreliable and rarely real-time
- Owners lack lightweight booking tools without payment liability

## The Opportunity
- Players want fast, mobile-first booking
- Owners need a simple reservation engine without payment processing
- Curated inventory can seed supply before full owner onboarding

## The Solution
- Unified discovery for curated and reservable courts
- Single booking flow with near-real-time availability
- P2P payment confirmation without handling money

## Core Users
- Players: find courts, view details, reserve quickly
- Organization owners: manage courts, slots, and confirmations
- Admins: curate supply and approve claims

## Product Experience
- Location search with list + map
- Court detail with amenities, photos, pricing
- Slot selection with clear availability states

## Reservation Engine
- Explicit time slots per court
- Status lifecycle: created → awaiting payment → confirmed/expired
- 15‑minute hold window for paid reservations

## Payments Without Liability
- External payment (GCash, bank, cash)
- Player marks as paid + uploads proof
- Owner confirms to finalize booking

## Court Claiming Workflow
- Curated courts are discoverable from day one
- Owners submit claim requests
- Admin approval converts to reservable courts

## Supply Strategy
- Manual curation to bootstrap inventory
- Claiming flow converts supply into managed venues
- Multi‑location organizations supported

## Monetization (MVP)
- Single banner ad placement on discovery page
- Non‑intrusive, no tracking required

## Differentiation
- Player-first UX, mobile-first design
- Payment-neutral P2P confirmation flow
- Audit trails for disputes and compliance

## Tech & Data Integrity
- Next.js + tRPC + Supabase + Drizzle
- RLS-based tenant isolation
- Reservation + claim audit logs

## Success Metrics
- Reservation completion rate
- Search → detail conversion
- Detail → booking start
- Claim approval rate

## Ask / Next Steps
- Pilot venues for early supply
- Advertiser partners for MVP revenue
- Strategic support for launch

# Owner Onboarding Script

**Page to open:** `/owners/get-started`

---

## Phase 1: "Let's get you visible"

Open their venue dashboard together.

> "Let's start with what matters most — making your venue discoverable to players. We'll do this together right now."

### 1. Venue Details

Walk them through updating their venue profile — name, address, photos, amenities, contact info.

> "This is what players see when they find you. Complete photos, accurate address, listed amenities — this is your storefront."

### 2. Court Scheduling & Pricing

Add each court with day-specific operating hours and hourly rate bands.

> "You set your hours and pricing per court, per day. Different rates for morning vs. evening? No problem — the system handles time-of-day pricing."
>
> "A major improvement is coming soon — add-ons support. You'll be able to charge for lights, equipment rental, things like that. Separate line items, not baked into the hourly rate."

### 3. Featured Placement

Show them the landing page and the province filter.

> "As an early adopter, we'll feature your venue on the landing page and in the province filter. Players in your area see you first — that's part of the early adopter perk."

### 4. QR Code

Generate their venue QR code.

> "Here's your QR code. Post it at your court entrance, put it on your Facebook page, share it in your Viber groups. Anyone who scans it lands directly on your booking page — no searching needed."

## Phase 2: "Now let's set up your schedule"

Open the Availability Studio section on the page. Point to the block type legend.

> "This is your timeline — your entire schedule in one place. Desktop shows the full week, mobile shows one day. Everything is managed with blocks."

Walk through each block type:

| Color | Block | What to say |
|---|---|---|
| Green (dashed) | **Available** | "Green means open. These are the slots players can book through the app. Pricing follows your hourly rate rules automatically." |
| Gray | **Maintenance** | "Gray means blocked. Repairs, cleaning, private events — players can't book these. Set it and forget it." |
| Light orange (dashed) | **Walk-in** | "Light orange with a dashed border — you're holding this time for walk-ins. When someone shows up and pays, you convert it to a confirmed guest booking with one tap." |
| Medium orange | **Guest Booking** | "Solid orange — a confirmed booking you created directly. For your suki, phone callers, anyone who doesn't use the app. It's confirmed the moment you create it." |

> "Green is open, gray is blocked, orange is reserved. You can read your whole day at a glance — no double-bookings, the system handles that automatically."

### Guest Booking Scenarios

Walk through the three guest scenarios:

**Suki calls to reserve:**
1. Open the Availability Studio and pick the time slot
2. Select their guest profile (or create one — name, phone, email)
3. Booking is immediately confirmed. No app needed on their end.
4. They pay you directly through your usual channels. Done.

**Walk-in arrives (block already set):**
1. You already have a walk-in block on the timeline for this hour
2. Customer arrives and pays at the counter
3. Tap the walk-in block → "Convert to guest booking"
4. Select existing profile or create a new one. Block becomes a confirmed reservation.

**New walk-in, no block:**
1. Open the Availability Studio and pick an open slot
2. Choose "Guest booking" and enter their name
3. Booking created and confirmed in one step. Profile saved for next time.

> "Whether bookings come through the app, by phone, or at the front desk — everything lives in one timeline."

## Phase 3: "Let's simulate a booking"

Scroll to the reservation flow section. Walk through each tab:

### Free Court
**Flow:** Pending → You Accept → Confirmed

> "A player finds your court and requests a booking. You tap Accept — done. Confirmed instantly. No payment step because the court is free."
>
> "You have a 45-minute window to accept or reject. If you don't respond, the request expires and the slot opens back up."

### Paid (Online)
**Flow:** Pending → Awaiting Payment → Payment Marked → Confirmed

> "You accept the request, which starts a 45-minute payment window. The player pays you directly — GCash, Maya, bank transfer — and marks it in the app. You tap Confirm Payment to finalize."
>
> "Every step is timed. If the player doesn't pay in time, the reservation expires and the slot reopens automatically. No manual cleanup."

### Paid (Offline)
**Flow:** Pending → Confirm Paid Offline → Confirmed

> "The player already paid you directly — cash, GCash, whatever your usual channels are. You tap Confirm Paid Offline — one action, jumps straight to confirmed."
>
> "This is for your suki, walk-ins who already paid, or anyone who handles payment through your traditional channels."

### Guest Booking
**Flow:** You Create Booking → Confirmed

> "A regular calls or shows up. Open the Availability Studio, pick the time, select their guest profile — immediately confirmed. No app needed on their end."
>
> "Guest bookings keep your schedule accurate for all customers — not just those who use the app."

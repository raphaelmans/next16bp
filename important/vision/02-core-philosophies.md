# Core Philosophies

Five guiding principles that inform every product, design, and copy decision.

## 1. Speedy Discoverability

Finding a court should take seconds, not minutes of tab-hopping. Search by city, sport, or amenities and see results immediately. Speed is a core value — in page load, in search, in the path from "I want to play" to "I know where to go."

## 2. Player-First, Always

Every feature starts with the player's need. Venues benefit because players benefit — more discoverable courts mean more foot traffic. We never optimize for venue revenue at the expense of player experience.

## 3. No Vendor Lock-In

Venues keep full control. Their listing, their pricing, their payment methods, their operations. KudosCourts adds visibility — it doesn't replace anything. A venue can stop using the platform tomorrow and lose nothing except discoverability.

## 4. Honest Information

Real player reviews from people who actually played there. Live availability when venues choose to manage it. No fake reviews, no inflated ratings, no hidden fees. If we don't have availability data, we say so — we don't fake it.

## 5. Community Over Marketplace

KudosCourts is a discovery platform, not a transaction layer. We connect players with courts and with each other. Revenue comes from the value of discovery, not from inserting ourselves into every transaction.

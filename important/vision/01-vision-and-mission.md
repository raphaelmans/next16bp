# Vision & Mission

## Vision

Every sports court in the Philippines, discoverable in seconds.

## Mission

Help Filipino players find the right court fast — by city, sport, reviews, and availability — while giving venues visibility and full control without fees or lock-in.

## Player Pain → Promise

**Pain:** Finding a court means scrolling through dozens of Facebook pages, checking separate reservation sites for each venue, reading scattered reviews across multiple platforms, and messaging owners one by one hoping for a reply.

**Promise:** One search surfaces every court in your area with real player reviews, photos, amenities, and availability — when venues manage it.

## Venue Pain → Promise

**Pain:** Players searching online can't find your venue. You rely on word-of-mouth, Facebook posts, and repeat customers.

**Promise:** Get found by every player searching in your city. Keep full control of your listing, pricing, and operations. No subscription fees, no commissions, no lock-in.

## Audience Split

- 80% Player — discovery, reviews, availability, community
- 20% Venue Owner — visibility, control, ease of setup

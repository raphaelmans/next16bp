# Venue Verification Outreach (Cold Email + Call Script)

Use this doc as a reference for outreach to venue owners/operators when we have already curated their venue/courts in KudosCourts.

## Quick Context (1-liner)

We list courts for discovery first, then invite owners to get verified and turn on reservations.

## Personalization Checklist

- Venue name: `{{venue_name}}`
- City/area: `{{city}}`
- Sport focus: `{{sport}}`
- Current listing URL (if available): `{{listing_url}}`
- Best contact channel (phone/Facebook/Instagram): `{{preferred_channel}}`

## Core Pitch (Short)

- We curated `{{venue_name}}` so players can discover it.
- Getting verified makes your venue high priority (trusted + promoted) and unlocks reservations.
- Verification protects owners: we validate phone + social links (and docs if needed) so no one can claim a venue they don't run.
- Early offer:
  - First 100 verified venues: Basic plan free for 6 months (reservation engine included).
  - Subsequent verified venues: 1 month Basic free trial.
- Opt-out friendly: if you don't want to be listed, we remove it immediately.

## Pricing (Reference)

- Free: listing/discovery (read-only), no reservations.
- Basic: PHP 500 / venue / month (or ~$10) to enable the reservation engine.

## Cold Email (Version A - Ultra Short)

Subject options:

- `Quick verification for {{venue_name}}`
- `We listed {{venue_name}} (opt-out anytime)`
- `Make {{venue_name}} bookable in 10 minutes`

Body:

Hi {{first_name}},

We added a curated listing for {{venue_name}} in KudosCourts so players can discover your courts.

If you're the owner/operator, we can verify the venue (phone + social links; docs if needed). Verified venues get priority on the platform and can turn on reservations.

Early offer: first 100 verified venues get 6 months of our Basic plan free (reservation engine included). After that, verified venues still get a 1-month free trial.

Would you like me to send the verification link, or should we remove the listing?

Thanks,
{{your_name}}
{{your_title}} | KudosCourts
{{your_phone}} | {{your_email}}

## Follow-up Sequence (3 Touches)

Touch 1 (48 hours later):

"Just checking if you're the right person for {{venue_name}}. Want the verification link, or should we remove the listing?"

Touch 2 (5-7 days later):

"We have an early offer for verified venues (first 100 get 6 months free). Still interested in making {{venue_name}} bookable?"

Touch 3 (final):

"No worries if not. Reply REMOVE and we'll take down the listing right away."

## Phone / WhatsApp Script (60-90 seconds)

"Hi, is this {{first_name}}? I'm {{your_name}} from KudosCourts. We help players discover courts and help venues get bookable.

We created a curated, read-only listing for {{venue_name}} so people can find it. If you're the owner/operator, we can verify ownership so nobody can fake-claim the venue.

Verification is quick: we confirm your phone number and official social links (and if needed, a document). Verified venues get priority and can turn on our reservation engine.

Right now we're onboarding early venues: first 100 verified get 6 months of the Basic plan free; after that it's still a 1-month free trial. After trial, Basic is PHP 500 per venue per month.

Would you like the verification link, or would you prefer we remove the listing?"

## Common Objections (Fast Replies)

"Why are you listing us without asking?"

- "We curate public court info to help players discover venues. The listing is read-only until verified, and we remove it immediately if you prefer not to be listed."

"We already take bookings via Facebook / phone."

- "Great. The reservation engine just standardizes requests and reduces back-and-forth. Verification also prevents someone else from claiming your venue on the platform."

"What do you need to verify?"

- "Usually phone + official social links is enough. If there's any ambiguity, we may request a simple document to confirm ownership/operation."

"How long does it take?"

- "Typically 5-10 minutes for the owner steps; review turnaround depends on queue, but we prioritize early venues."

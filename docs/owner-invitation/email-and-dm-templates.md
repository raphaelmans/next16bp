- “Quick question about {{venue_name}}”
- “Claim {{venue_name}} on KudosCourts?”
- “Make {{venue_name}} bookable (free for venues)”

Body:

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m {{your_name}} from KudosCourts. We help players discover courts and we provide a free reservation system for venues.

{{#if listing_url}}
We created a read-only listing for {{venue_name}} here:
{{listing_url}}
{{/if}}

If you manage {{venue_name}}, you can claim and verify the venue to enable online reservations (you control when bookings go live).

Start here: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

Reply “VERIFY” and I’ll point you to the fastest path.

Thanks,  
{{your_name}}

PS: We don’t process payments—venues keep their existing payment methods.

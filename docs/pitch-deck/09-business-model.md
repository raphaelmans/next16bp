# Business Model (Draft)

Near-term focus

- Win on discovery and booking conversion (player-first).
- Convert supply through curated listings -> claim -> verify -> enable bookings.

Monetization options (choose/sequence)

- Sponsored placements / featured venues in discovery.
- Owner subscription for advanced ops (multi-user staff, analytics, automation).
- Booking fees / payments (only if the product later chooses to take on payment processing).
- Advertising (if/when it fits the UX).

MVP

- Monetization is intentionally flexible while we validate supply + repeat bookings.

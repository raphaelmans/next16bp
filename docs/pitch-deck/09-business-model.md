# Business Model (Draft)

Core promise

- Free for venues: core listing + reservation tools.
- No payment processing: venues keep their existing payment methods; players pay venues directly.

Near-term focus

- Win on discovery and booking conversion.
- Convert supply through curated listings -> claim -> verify -> enable reservations.

Monetization (aligned, transparent)

- Advertising / sponsored placements in discovery (when it fits the UX).
- Optional future: advanced owner ops (multi-user staff, analytics, automation) if the product needs it.
- Partner programs with sports equipment brands, tournaments, and communities.

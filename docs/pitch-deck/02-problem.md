# Problem

Players

- Discovery is fragmented: social pages, chat threads, screenshots, and outdated pins.
- Availability is unclear: “Is this court actually available?” often requires messaging/calls.
- Booking is inconsistent: every venue has a different process, rules, and rates.

Owners

- Scheduling is manual: spreadsheets + DMs + phone calls = errors and double-booking risk.
- Rates and policies are hard to communicate consistently.
- "Going digital" typically means expensive custom software or awkward tools.

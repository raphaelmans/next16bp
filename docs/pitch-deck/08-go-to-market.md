# Go-To-Market (Draft)

Wedge

- Start with one sport focus (pickleball), multi-sport ready by design.
- Seed supply through curated venue listings to win discovery early.

Conversion loop

- Curate courts for high-intent venues (discoverable, read-only).
- Players use discovery and drive demand.
- Proactive venue outreach: claim your listing + get verified.
- Verified + enabled venues publish real inventory, improving the discovery experience.

Verification offer (early supply)

- Verified venues are prioritized (ranking + onboarding support).
- We act as a third party to validate ownership (phone + social links; docs as needed) to prevent fake owners.
- First 100 verifications: reservation engine free for the first 6 months.
- If a venue does not want to be listed, we remove it immediately on request.

Channels (examples)

- Local club partnerships, tournaments, community groups.
- SEO for "courts near me" and city/province discovery pages.
- Venue outreach: "claim your place" and "get bookable" onboarding.

## Ready-to-post examples (Owners)

### Post 1 — Hook the pain (DMs)

Bookings stuck in DMs?

If you’re managing court reservations through messages, spreadsheets, or a shared calendar, you’ve probably dealt with the same problems:

- double bookings
- missed messages
- constant back-and-forth

KudosCourts is a free reservation system for venues in the Philippines. Claim and verify your venue, publish availability, and manage reservations in one dashboard.

Start here: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

### Post 2 — “Free” + clarity

KudosCourts is free for venues.

That means:

- no subscription fees for core listing + reservations
- venues keep their existing payment methods
- we don’t process payments

Players get clearer availability. Venues get fewer booking conflicts.

List your venue: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

### Post 3 — Verification trust

Why do we ask venues to verify?

Because it protects you.
Verification prevents fake owners from claiming your venue and gives players a trusted reservation experience.

Claim + verify here: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

### Post 4 — Import bookings

Already have existing bookings?

You can import bookings (ICS/CSV/XLSX) to block those times and prevent double bookings before you go live.

Get started: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

### Post 5 — Founder transparency

I’m building KudosCourts to make sports court reservations accessible—especially for small and newly opened venues.

A lot of venues still rely on DMs and spreadsheets because software has historically been expensive. KudosCourts is free for venues, and we plan to sustain the platform through ads/sponsored placements—not subscriptions.

If you manage a venue, I’d love your feedback:
https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started

# Social Media Portrayal (Facebook + Instagram)

This playbook defines how we present KudosCourts publicly across FB/IG.

## Goal (Next 30 days)

Acquire venue owners/operators:

- List/claim venue
- Verify ownership
- Enable reservations (when ready)

## One-liner (use everywhere)

Court discovery for players. Free reservation system for venues.

## Primary CTA (Owners)

- https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started
- CTA text: “List your venue — free for venues” / “Claim your listing”

## Secondary CTA (Players)

- https://www.kudoscourts.com/courts

## Non-negotiables (brand truth)

- “Free for venues” = the platform is free for venues. It does NOT mean court time is free.
- No payment processing: venues keep their existing payment methods; players pay venues directly.
- Verification exists to prevent fake owners and protect venues.
- Use “rates” or “price” (venue charges). Avoid “Pricing” as a SaaS-plan concept.

## Voice & tone

Friendly, professional, clean, confident. No hype. No fake urgency.

(See: `business-contexts/kudoscourts-design-system.md`)

## Audience segments (FB/IG)

1. Venue owners/operators (primary)
   - Pain: DMs + spreadsheets + calendars → double bookings + admin overhead
   - Want: simple setup, control, trust, fewer conflicts
2. Players (supporting)
   - Pain: unclear availability, slow replies, inconsistent booking flow
   - Want: discover courts + reserve with less back-and-forth

## Content pillars (with the psychology behind them)

1. “Bookings stuck in DMs?” (Loss aversion, Contrast effect)
2. Double-booking prevention (Loss aversion)
3. Free reservation system (Zero-price effect)
4. Verified owners + trust pipeline (Authority bias, Social proof)
5. No payment processing (Regret aversion: removes “what if…” fear)
6. Player discovery (Mere exposure effect; makes owners want to be present)
7. Founder transparency (Liking/similarity; builds trust ethically)

## Visual system (match the product)

- Minimalist bento, warm neutrals, generous whitespace
- Primary teal for the main CTA only; orange for highlights/links
- Use product UI screenshots and simple diagrams; avoid busy posters

## Proof rules (critical)

- Do not invent metrics (“thousands of players…”).
- Only claim what we can show or verify (feature screenshots, real counts, verified badge).
- Prefer: “players are already searching” over unverified numeric claims.

## Profile copy (examples)

Instagram bio (Option A)

- “Court discovery for players. Free reservation system for venues in the Philippines.”

Instagram bio (Option B)

- “List your venue for free. Get verified. Accept reservations. No payment processing.”

Pinned post (FB) template

- Hook: “Venue owners: still taking bookings in DMs?”
- Value: “KudosCourts helps you publish availability + accept reservations in one dashboard.”
- Trust: “Claim + verify to protect your venue.”
- Clarity: “Free for venues. No payment processing.”
- CTA: “Start here: https://www.kudoscourts.com/owners/get-started”

## Comment + DM flow

Default “small commitment” CTA (Commitment & consistency):

- “Comment VERIFY and I’ll send the link.”
- “Reply VERIFY and I’ll send the link.”

If they ask “What’s the catch?”

- “Core listing + reservations are free for venues. We aim to sustain through ads/sponsored placements—not subscriptions.”

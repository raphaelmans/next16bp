# Open Play - User Stories

Context: Player-hosted Open Play sessions attached to a reservation, inspired by Reclub-style "meets" (requested/waitlist/confirmed), but adapted to KudosCourts constraints.

## Domain Overview

Open Play lets a player who has reserved a court turn that reservation into a joinable session so other players can discover it on the venue page and request to join.

Key product decision:
- Open Plays are publicly discoverable and joinable only after the underlying reservation is `CONFIRMED`.

Reservation flow UX (important):
- Creating an Open Play must NOT disrupt the reservation flow.
- After booking, the user should continue to the normal reservation flow (payment page if needed, otherwise reservation detail).
- Open Play should be surfaced from the reservation detail page via a clear info card/section with a CTA to view/copy the Open Play link.
- Avoid automatic redirects to `/open-play/[openPlayId]` from booking/reservation flows.

Reservations list UX (important):
- On `/reservations`, each reservation item should clearly indicate whether it has an Open Play.
- Provide a fast path to configure Open Play from the list (without leaving the reservation flow):
  - If Open Play exists: “View Open Play”
  - If missing: “Set up Open Play” (takes user to reservation detail and opens the Open Play setup dialog)

Open Play host ops UX:
- Open Play detail should support near-real-time updates for Requests/Waitlist.
- Provide either:
  - A manual Refresh button (host-only), and/or
  - Polling every 15 seconds while the Open Play is upcoming and ACTIVE.

Cost sharing (primary use case):
- Open Play is commonly used to share the reservation cost with friends/other players.
- MVP approach: host can optionally add cost-sharing info (suggested split per player + payment instructions/link).
- Payments remain off-platform (KudosCourts is not a payment processor). Host confirms participants after payment is received.
- Suggested split should consider headcount (e.g., split reservation total across expected number of players; default to Open Play capacity/max players).

Reference (Reclub-style meets + off-platform payments/receipts):
- https://reclub.co/platform/meets
- https://help.reclub.co/hc/reclub-help/articles/1765845748-how-do-payments-work-in-recub

    - otherwise -> reservation detail page

  - Optional: toast success “Open Play created. You can view it from Reservation Details.”

## US-80-01: Host marks booking as Open Play at checkout

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **player (host)**, I want to **mark my booking as an Open Play while I confirm the reservation** so that **other players can join once my reservation is confirmed**.

### Acceptance Criteria

#### Mark as Open Play

- Given I am on the booking confirmation step for a venue
- When I enable a "Host as Open Play" option and confirm the booking
- Then an Open Play is created for that reservation (or scheduled to be created)

#### Preserve reservation flow

- Given I enabled "Host as Open Play" at checkout
- When the reservation is created
- Then I continue the reservation flow (payment page if required, otherwise reservation detail)
- And I can access the Open Play from the reservation detail page via a dedicated Open Play card/section

#### Visibility gating (confirmed only)

- Given my reservation is not yet confirmed
- When I view the venue Open Play list
- Then my Open Play is not publicly listed
- And other players cannot join it

- Given my reservation becomes confirmed
- When other players view the venue Open Play list
- Then my Open Play appears as joinable

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Open Play creation fails after reservation is created | Reservation remains valid; I see a clear message and a way to finish creating the Open Play from the reservation details. |
| Reservation expires/cancels before confirmation | The Open Play never becomes discoverable/joinable. |

### Form Fields (if applicable)

| Field | Type | Required |
|-------|------|----------|
| Host as Open Play | boolean | No |
| Max players | number | Yes |
| Join policy | select | Yes |
| Note | textarea | No |

## US-80-02: Host converts an existing reservation into an Open Play

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **player (host)**, I want to **convert my existing reservation into an Open Play** so that **I can invite others to join my session**.

### Acceptance Criteria

#### Convert reservation

- Given I have a future reservation that I own
- When I choose "Create Open Play" from the reservation details
- Then the system creates an Open Play attached to that reservation
- And I can view the Open Play details page

#### Prevent duplicates

- Given I already created an Open Play for this reservation
- When I try to create another one
- Then I am shown the existing Open Play instead of creating a duplicate

#### Confirmed-only discoverability

- Given the reservation is not confirmed
- Then the Open Play is not publicly discoverable and not joinable

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Reservation is in the past | "Create Open Play" is hidden or disabled with an explanation. |
| Reservation is cancelled/expired | "Create Open Play" is not allowed. |

## US-80-03: Viewer browses Open Plays from a venue page toggle

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **viewer**, I want to **toggle a venue page between booking and Open Plays** so that **I can quickly see if there are hosted Open Play sessions at that venue**.

### Acceptance Criteria

#### Toggle between modes

- Given I am on a venue detail page
- When I switch to the "Open Play" view
- Then the booking UI is replaced with an Open Play list UI (client-rendered)

#### Show upcoming Open Plays

- Given there are upcoming Open Plays for this venue
- Then I see a list of them with key info (time, sport, available spots, host)

#### Empty state

- Given there are no upcoming Open Plays
- Then I see an empty state with a CTA to host an Open Play

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Slow network | Show skeleton/loading state without breaking the venue page. |
| Venue is curated (not bookable) | Open Play view still works (listing only). |

## US-80-05: Player requests to join an Open Play

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **player (joiner)**, I want to **request to join an Open Play** so that **I can participate in the session if approved**.

### Acceptance Criteria

#### Request to join

- Given I am signed in and viewing an Open Play that is confirmed, active, and in the future
- When I submit a join request
- Then my status becomes "Requested" (or "Waitlisted" if full)
- And I see a confirmation message

#### Auto-join option

- Given the Open Play is configured for auto-join
- When I request to join and there is capacity
- Then I become "Confirmed" immediately

#### Prevent joining own Open Play

- Given I am the host
- When I attempt to join
- Then the join action is not available

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Open Play is not yet reservation-confirmed | Joining is blocked with a clear message. |
| Already requested/confirmed | Duplicate requests are prevented; show current status. |

| Field | Type | Required |
|-------|------|----------|
| Optional message to host | textarea | No |

## US-80-06: Host reviews and manages participants

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **player (host)**, I want to **review join requests and manage who is confirmed/waitlisted** so that **I can control capacity and session quality**.

### Acceptance Criteria

#### Review participants

- Given I am the host viewing my Open Play
- Then I can see participant statuses (Requested, Confirmed, Waitlisted)

#### Approve/decline/waitlist

- Given I have pending join requests
- When I approve a request
- Then the player becomes Confirmed (if capacity allows)

- When I decline a request
- Then the player becomes Declined

#### Capacity enforcement

- Given the Open Play is at max capacity
- When I attempt to confirm an additional player
- Then I am prevented and can place them in Waitlisted instead

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Two approvals happen quickly | System still enforces capacity; one may be waitlisted. |
| Host reservation becomes cancelled | Open Play becomes non-joinable and shows status. |

## US-80-10: Open Play group + in-app group chat

**Status:** Draft

### Story

As a **player (host or joiner)**, I want **an in-app group chat for the Open Play** so that **participants can coordinate without external apps**.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Given I am confirmed in an Open Play
- When I open the Open Play detail page
- Then I can access the group chat for that Open Play
- And I can send/read messages from other confirmed participants and the host

#### Access control

- Given I am not confirmed (Requested/Waitlisted)
- Then I cannot access the group chat yet

- Given I leave the Open Play or I am declined/removed
- Then I lose access to the group chat

### Edge Cases

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Open Play is closed/cancelled | Chat may remain viewable to confirmed participants, but joining stays disabled. |
| Host removes a participant | The participant loses access to the chat. |

### Notes

- Adding player-to-player chat introduces moderation/safety requirements (reporting, blocking, abuse handling). MVP can start with minimal tooling but should not ship without a basic abuse path.

## Verification (Manual QA)

Happy paths:
- Host creates Open Play at checkout (free reservation): can choose AUTO; joiners can auto-confirm; confirmed can chat.
- Host creates Open Play at checkout (paid reservation): joinPolicy forced to REQUEST; joiners request; host confirms after payment; confirmed can chat.
- Venue detail toggle shows Open Plays only after reservation CONFIRMED.

Edge cases:
- Open Play link opened before reservation CONFIRMED: non-host sees NOT_FOUND; host sees banner.
- Join after start time: join disabled in UI; server rejects if attempted.
- Full session: joiner becomes WAITLISTED; host can promote from waitlist.
- Cancelled session: status visible; join disabled; detail remains viewable.

Regression:
- Chat membership removal on leave / de-confirm still works.
- SEO metadata functions do not throw for NOT_FOUND sessions.

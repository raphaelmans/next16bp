# Place Claiming - User Stories

## Overview

The Place Claiming domain defines how curated places (created by admins for discovery bootstrapping) can be claimed by organizations.

Curated places show full place details, explicit court inventory (multi-sport), and public contact information, but are not bookable until claimed and approved.

Once a claim is approved, the place becomes reservable under the organization, and the existing courts are transferred implicitly with the place.
